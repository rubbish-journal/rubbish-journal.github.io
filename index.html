About the Journal

"Just as the circular economy transforms waste into resources, RUBBISH transforms discarded academic work into published scholarship."

Aims & Scope

RUBBISH: Journal of Waste & Circular Economy is an open-access, peer-reviewed academic journal that provides a publication platform for scholarly work that has been rejected, abandoned, or otherwise overlooked by the mainstream academic publishing ecosystem.

The journal is founded on the principle that knowledge production generates a significant volume of unpublished material — including null results, failed experiments, rejected manuscripts, and incomplete drafts — that nevertheless carries scholarly value. By providing a venue for such work, RUBBISH aims to reduce "academic waste" and promote a more transparent, inclusive model of scholarly communication.

We welcome submissions across all disciplines. Accepted article types include original research articles, reports of failed experiments and null results, previously rejected manuscripts, short communications, and methodological reflections.

Open Access Policy

All content published in RUBBISH is immediately and freely available to all readers worldwide. The journal charges no Article Processing Charges (APCs). We believe that open access to scholarly work — particularly work that has been excluded from conventional publication channels — is essential for the advancement of knowledge.

Review Process

All submissions are subject to editorial review. The editorial team evaluates manuscripts for scholarly integrity, clarity of presentation, and relevance to the journal's scope. Our review process is designed to be efficient and supportive, with a target turnaround time of 14 days from submission to initial decision.

For Authors

Submission Guidelines

RUBBISH welcomes submissions from researchers at all career stages and across all disciplines. Manuscripts may be written in Chinese or English. We particularly encourage submissions in Chinese to promote multilingual scholarship.

Article Types

Research Articles — Original research, including studies with null or negative results. No maximum word count.

Failed Experiments — Structured reports documenting experimental approaches that did not produce expected outcomes, with emphasis on methodological lessons.

Rejected Manuscripts — Papers that have been rejected by other venues. Authors are encouraged to include a brief reflection on the review process.

Short Communications — Brief reports, including preliminary findings, methodological notes, and reflections on academic practice.

How to Submit

Submissions should be prepared using the journal's manuscript template and submitted through our online submission system.

Review Process

All submitted manuscripts undergo editorial review. The editorial team assesses each submission for scholarly integrity, clarity, and alignment with the journal's scope. Authors will receive an initial decision within approximately 14 days of submission. RUBBISH operates a supportive review process; our goal is to help authors improve and publish their work, not to serve as an additional gatekeeping mechanism.

Publication Ethics

RUBBISH is committed to upholding standards of publication ethics. Plagiarism, data fabrication, and data falsification are not acceptable. Authors submitting previously rejected manuscripts must confirm that the work is their own and has not been published elsewhere. The journal follows the general principles outlined by the Committee on Publication Ethics (COPE).
